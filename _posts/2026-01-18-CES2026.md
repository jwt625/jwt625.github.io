I did not sign up for CES ahead of time, and had to do it when I'm already in the queue for picking up the badge. Good news is that the queue was really long and it took more than 30 min of waiting. Bad news is that the network is horrible, I could barely get any normal cell signal. Good news is that there are free shuttle bus nearby that came with free onboard wifi which I managed to get on. Then bad news is that after the registration there is an up to 3 days of admin check process to make sure you are actually from industry. Good news is that I jsut asked the kind lady to find my info in the system and print the badge anyway. She didn't even scan my QR code because I have not got one yet. I should have cancelled the registration afterwards lol.
